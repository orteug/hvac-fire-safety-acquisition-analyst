# HVAC & Fire Safety Acquisition Analyst
### A Folder-Based AI Specialist for Claude Projects

> **Get a structured deal screen on any HVAC or Fire & Life Safety business — in under 5 minutes.**

---

## What This Is

A folder-based AI specialist you drop into a Claude Project. Once it's in, Claude becomes a disciplined acquisition analyst for small HVAC and Fire & Life Safety businesses — operator-owned trades companies doing $500K–$5M in annual revenue.

Built for **micro-PE operator-buyers**: people who want to own and run a trades business, not flip it.

**You get:**
- Structured deal screens with a clear PURSUE vs. PASS verdict (not a wishy-washy "it depends")
- Risk flags ranked by severity (deal-killers vs. negotiation points)
- PE competitive context — who else is calling your target and how to compete
- Data gap identification before you waste time on bad deals
- Output calibrated to 2025–2026 transaction data and current SBA rules

---

## Setup (3 Steps)

**1. Create a Claude Project**
Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai), create a new Project. Name it `Acquisition Analyst — Trades` or similar.

**2. Upload This Folder**
Upload all files into the Project's knowledge base — maintain the `reference/` subfolder structure.

**3. Start Analyzing Deals**
Open a conversation inside the Project and paste what you know about a deal.

---

## Minimum Input to Get Started

- Business type (HVAC, Fire Safety, or both)
- Annual revenue
- Asking price
- Owner's role in day-to-day operations
- Rough headcount

---

## Example Prompt

```
HVAC company in Austin, TX. $1.8M revenue, owner says SDE is around $310K.
Asking $1.1M (3.5x SDE). Owner has been there 18 years, wants to retire in 12
months. He runs sales — 3 techs and a dispatcher handling ops. 55%
residential, 45% light commercial. About 280 active maintenance agreements.
No single customer over 12% of revenue.

Run a deal screen.
```

---

## Output Structure

Every deal screen includes:
1. **Deal Summary** — What the business is, plainly
2. **Initial Verdict** — PURSUE / PROCEED WITH CAUTION / PASS + one-line rationale
3. **Strengths** — What's working in the deal's favor
4. **Risk Flags** — 🔴 Critical → 🟡 Moderate → 🟢 Monitor
5. **PE Competitive Context** — Who else is looking and what to do about it
6. **Data Gaps** — What to request before moving forward
7. **Next Step** — Exactly what to do next

---

## Advanced Modes

| Prompt | Mode |
|--------|------|
| "Run a deep dive..." | Adds financing scenario, operator dependency score, deal structure suggestion |
| "Red flag scan only" | Returns only applicable flags, ranked by severity |
| "Does this work with SBA 7(a)?" | Runs DSCR sanity check at current asking price |
