# Shared Screening Criteria
## Context Layer 3 · Applies to Both HVAC and F&LS Deals

> Thresholds calibrated to micro-PE operator-buyer deals in $500K–$5M revenue range.
> Domain-specific thresholds: see `hvac-criteria.md` and `fls-criteria.md`.
> Last Updated: May 2026

---

## Financial Health

| Metric | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| SDE/EBITDA Margin | >20% | 12–20% | <12% |
| Revenue Trend (3yr) | Growing or stable | Flat ±5% | Declining >10% YOY |
| Revenue Concentration (top client) | <15% | 15–30% | >30% |
| Revenue Concentration (top 3 clients) | <30% | 30–50% | >50% |
| Asking Multiple (on SDE) | <3.5x | 3.5–4.0x | >4.0x (per thesis ceiling) |
| Revenue Multiple | <0.6x | 0.6–1.0x | >1.0x |

Notes:
- SDE = Net income + owner salary/benefits + non-cash + one-time expenses
- Verify SDE via 3-year P&L, not seller self-report
- Revenue concentration tolerance is higher when accounts are under long-term contracts

## Owner-Operator Dependency

The #1 failure mode in trades acquisitions.

| Area | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| Owner's operational role | Strategic/admin only | Partial field work | Primary tech or only salesperson |
| Client relationships | Held by team or systems | Mixed | All flow through owner |
| Institutional knowledge | Documented SOPs | Partially documented | All in owner's head |
| Transition willingness | 12+ months | 6–12 months | <6 months or unwilling |

Key diagnostic questions:
1. If owner took a 30-day vacation today, what breaks?
2. Which clients would leave if the owner left?
3. Who is the owner's #2 — and could they run daily ops for 90 days?

## Team & Human Capital

| Metric | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Total certified technicians | 4+ | 2–3 | 1 (owner only) |
| Lead tech / field supervisor exists | Yes | Informal | No |
| Average tech tenure | >3 years | 1–3 years | <1 year |
| Non-compete / key-person agreements | In place | Partial | None |

---

## Geographic & Market Position

| Factor | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Market position | Dominant local or top 3 | Known but not dominant | Undifferentiated |
| Service radius | Compact, high-density | Moderate | Sprawling, thin coverage |
| Population trend | Growing | Stable | Declining |
| Competitive landscape | Fragmented | Mixed | One dominant regional chain |

---

## Asset Condition

| Asset | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|-------|-------|---------|----------|
| Fleet age | <5 years average | 5–8 years | >8 years or deferred maintenance |
| Equipment owned vs. leased | Owned or favorable leases | Mix | Operating leases with escalators |
| Real property | Leased with favorable terms | Month-to-month | Owner-occupied (lease-to-close complexity) |

---

## Legal & Compliance

Any of the following = automatic deeper review before proceeding:
- Active litigation (vendor, customer, employee)
- Workers' comp claims pattern suggesting unsafe job sites
- Customer complaints on BBB, Google, or licensing boards
- Owner won't sign non-compete (per thesis: walk)

---

## Recurring Revenue Quality

| Metric | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| % of revenue from recurring sources | >35% | 15–35% | <15% |
| Agreement auto-renewal rate | >80% | 60–80% | <60% |
| Install vs. service revenue split | <60% install | 60–75% install | >75% install |

High install revenue = revenue volatility leading indicator.
