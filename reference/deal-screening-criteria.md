Evaluation criteria used in every deal screen. Thresholds based on micro-PE acquisition standards for operator-buyer deals in the $500K–$5M revenue range.

## 1. Financial Health

| Metric | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| SDE/EBITDA Margin | >20% | 12–20% | <12% |
| Revenue Trend (3yr) | Growing or stable | Flat ±5% | Declining >10% YOY |
| Revenue Concentration (top client) | <15% | 15–30% | >30% |
| Revenue Concentration (top 3 clients) | <30% | 30–50% | >50% |
| Asking Multiple on SDE (HVAC) | <3.5x | 3.5–4.5x | >4.5x |
| Asking Multiple on EBITDA (F&LS inspection-heavy) | <4.5x | 4.5–5.5x | >5.5x |
| Revenue Multiple | <0.6x | 0.6–1.0x | >1.0x |

## 2. Recurring Revenue Quality

| Metric | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Service/maintenance agreement count | >200 HVAC; >50 commercial F&LS | 50–200 | <50 |
| % of revenue from recurring sources | >35% | 15–35% | <15% |
| Agreement auto-renewal rate | >80% | 60–80% | <60% |
| Install vs. service revenue split | <60% install | 60–75% install | >75% install |

Note: Maintenance agreement ARR is valued separately at 2x–3x in addition to the EBITDA multiple. See valuation-methodology.md.

## 3. Owner-Operator Dependency

| Dimension | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|------|-------|---------|----------|
| Owner's operational role | Strategic/admin only | Partial field work | Primary tech or only salesperson |
| Client relationships | Held by team or systems | Mixed | All flow through owner |
| Institutional knowledge | Documented SOPs | Partially documented | All in owner's head |
| Transition willingness | 12+ month overlap | 6–12 months | <6 months or unwilling |

Key questions: If owner took a 30-day vacation today, what breaks? Which clients would leave? Who is the #2?

## 4. Team & Human Capital

| Metric | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Total certified technicians | 4+ | 2–3 | 1 (owner only) |
| Lead technician / field supervisor | Yes | Informal | No |
| Average tech tenure | >3 years | 1–3 years | <1 year or high turnover |
| Non-compete / key-person agreements | In place | Partial | None |

## 5. Geographic & Market Position

| Factor | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|--------|-------|---------|----------|
| Market position | Dominant local or top 3 | Known but not dominant | Undifferentiated |
| Service radius | Compact, high-density | Moderate | Sprawling, thin coverage |
| Population trend | Growing | Stable | Declining |
| Competitive landscape | Fragmented | Mixed | Dominant chain or active PE platform |

## 6. Asset Condition

| Asset | Green | Caution | Red Flag |
|-------|-------|---------|----------|
| Fleet age | <5 years avg | 5–8 years | >8 years or deferred maintenance |
| Equipment owned vs. leased | Owned or favorable leases | Mix | Operating leases with escalators |
| Real property | Leased, favorable terms | Month-to-month | Owner-occupied (must negotiate separately) |

## 7. Legal & Compliance

Automatic deeper review before proceeding if any of the following:
- Active litigation (vendor, customer, employee)
- EPA refrigerant violations or unpermitted installations
- AHJ citations for Fire Safety work
- Unlicensed contractor history
- Workers' comp claims pattern suggesting systemic safety failures
